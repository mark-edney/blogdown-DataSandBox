The final project for the Machine Learning course for Johns Hopkins University Data Science Specialization. The project looks at the data collected for different smart fitness trackers. The project uses machine learning to classify the level of activity based on a wide variety of variables.

The individual steps are outlined in a paper published on RPubs here. It outlines the use of the following models:

  • Random forest
  • Generalized Boosted
  • Linear Discriminant
  • Combined
